PROBLEM STATEMENT

Unambiguously displaying municipal infrastructure data

We face the challenge of displaying data without resorting to voluminous quantities of potentially superfluous and ambiguous text for the express purpose of pontificating upon the matters at hand. To avoid confounding our clients with excess verbosity, which would hamper their education we have, after much deliberation and discussion, come to the most certain conclusion that a cartographic output would allow those individuals interested in our information to easily ascertain what they wish us to divulge
